Logo For my Portofoliowebsite

This logo was designed by me. It's made up of the letters m for maria and paula. Something I painted on the left side of the M was altered. With the intention of giving the M some life by adding a curve to the top. I connect the part per to the back of the M. If you see a little more depth, join the two letters like a 4. The bottom section looks like a signature drawing, and it seemed like a simple and good idea to implement.

Because I had no experience with illustrator, the process of creating this logo was kind of difficult. However, by watching some YouTube videos, I was able to gain a better understanding of this program. Despite my practice, I still can't find some functions. I see the video, but when I go to imitate, I often can't find the settings. This is my initial logo design. I intend to create additional prototypes in the future in order to improve the design of my logo for this portfolio.

Photography

We had to learn how to use a professional camera in this second part. It was a new experience for me. That was a lot of fun. Something I believe I lacked in this task was a clearer vision of how to photograph the tasks that They assigned to us. I also learned the difference between shutter speed and aperture, as well as what kind of angle to take based on the angle of light, ISO, and many other factors that are important when taking a photograph. I can say that this task has taught me a lot, and some of the tasks took me longer to understand how I should approach that photography.

Because I had never worked with the Indesign program before, it was a bit difficult for me to do the design and the document itself, the InDesign program showed many errors or the way they showed me on the internet when I tried to execute the same function, it didn't work for me, and so there were many more problems with this program. But now that I've finished my photography document, I can say that I know how, and there are many things about how this program works that I now find much easier to work on.

Lightroom and Photoshop I also had no prior experience with the Photoshop program; I had never spent time learning how it works, and for this task, I needed to learn how to work in Photoshop and improve my photographs. I came across some excellent tutorials that greatly aided me. When it comes to putting what I saw on YouTube into practice, you can see a better performance that I acquired in this period of time with Photoshop in my photography document.

It was easier to work with Lightroom because everything was visible and it wasn't as complicated as Photoshop or Indesign. There were some functions that I couldn't find, such as white balance; in order to complete the white balance task and see all of the information, I had to download another program that allowed me to see the white balance of the photos. I also use Lightroom a lot because all of my photographs are in cr2 format and I had to convert them to jpeg because indesign only accepts this jpeg format. I was able to learn a lot of different knowledge of all these programs by doing all of these tasks from the document on indesgin.
